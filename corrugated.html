Fresh Produce

Material and manufacturing specialized Packaging.

Our expertise in boxes used for cold chain supplies, specially for grape boxes, Banana Boxes, Mango, Vegetables, Citrus & other all types fruit & Vegetable boxes for Export to Europe, USA & other countries as well using domestically.

These fresh produce boxes are transit and store in cold supply chain and high humidity, we have to use Virgin KLB as well Nordic semi chemical fluting paper for box sustainability. The most important criteria are highly automized board manufacturing process thru water resistant glue recipe and fully automated board drying process.

The cold chain process needs high quality Kraft liner as well Specialized fluting medium, which we import from Nordic Countries like Finland, Sweden and Poland.

 

Lifestyle

Design and development specialized Packaging.

We are experts in packaging design solutions, we have inhouse design studio with automatic sample maker machine facility, we develop packaging, Fitments to give support with Structural support to protect external impacts with good aesthetics (especially delicate products, like Ceramics product, Kitchen Sink, Bathroom Fittings etc...). We have high-end software to create design of boxes structurally as well as thetically.

The major world-renowned brands like TOTO, Duravit, Acrylis , Viega etc are our valuable customers.

 

Other Segments

We pioneer in many other segments Like Frozen Food, Dyes and Intermediates, Pharmaceutical, Sanitary Ware, Tobacco Industry and many others.

Our Specialty to produce packaging.

We are exporting our boxes to Brazil, South Africa, Egypt, Thailand, Dubai, Australia and many other countries for many products,. Production capacity of our plant is up to 6500 MT per month, we have installed world-class printing + converting lines from Europe. These high-speed line with Auto loader + 6 color printing (including coating) + Rotary die cutter + counter ejector + Turntable + Breaker and bundling inline, box bundles are transfer up to vehicle thru automated conveyor without hand touching.
